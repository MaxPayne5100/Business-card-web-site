My name is Mykhailo Bordun and I’m a professional photographer.

“Photography is my muse", my true profession, and the thing that will undoubtedly drive me insane someday.

I don’t photograph things. I photograph the way they make me feel. Admittedly, it’s a bit of a strange concept. But it’s honest – and it’s the best way to describe my approach to the craft.

I have been very lucky to have had my photography appear in Architectural Digest, Country Life Magazine and Conde Naste Traveler.
